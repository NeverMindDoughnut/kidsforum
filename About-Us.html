About Us

The Kids' Forum team aims to provide a "worry-free" environment in which kids ages 5 and up are comfortable interacting with their peers. We have set up safety measures to ensure that only school age kids are able to access the site. Safety protocols, age verification, parental permission, and school or homeschool verifications have been set up by us as well. We are ranked #1 in the global community based on our product.
